SAINT JOSEPH'S EM HIGH SCHOOL

Saint Joseph's EM High School was established as the first English Medium High School in the year 199X by V. Joy Joseph in Singarayakonda. Our school has now completed three decades in the realm of education, during which the institution has fostered numerous achievers and inspirers who continue to make their mark across the globe today.


Welcome to Our School

At Our School, we take pride in our diverse and inclusive educational environment. Our classrooms are home to students ranging from nursery to the 10th grade, offering a comprehensive learning experience for every child. Our commitment to providing top-notch facilities is evident in our modern computer lab, well-equipped projector classrooms, and a spacious playground where students can not only learn but also play and grow.


What truly sets us apart is our exceptional team of dedicated teachers who are devoted to the all-round development of each and every child. With a focus on nurturing both academic excellence and personal growth, our teachers create a cool and welcoming atmosphere where students can thrive.


But that's not all! We understand the importance of access to education, which is why we offer bus transportation for nearby villages, ensuring that students from various areas have the opportunity to benefit from the high-quality education we provide.


Join us at Our School, where we foster a love for learning and holistic development in a supportive and inclusive community. Your child's journey towards success begins here.
